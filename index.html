Professional Therapist

We specialize in helping seniors and others unable to travel regain their mobility and independence by coming to your home.

Physio2U provides accessible therapy for all with in-home physiotherapy and kinesiology services for privacy, comfort and convenience throughout a person’s recovery. Our team specializes in treating people struggling with mobility and strength issues due to trauma, surgery, neurological conditions, as well as chronic health issues. We understand there are two sides of the care equation: the medical and the emotional. We focus on both to ensure an optimal road to recovery for you or a loved one all in the comfort of your own home.

Our Team

01

Physiotherapists

Our experienced team of licensed and insured Registered Physiotherapists have a wide range of skills and areas of expertise. When you call to book, we will match you with the best therapist suited to help your needs who is available in your geographic area.

02

Kinesiologists

Kinesiology is the science of human movement. Our kinesiologists are members of the British Columbia Association of Kinesiologists (BCAK). This means they have qualified professional standards, including a four-year degree (or equivalent) in Kinesiology. A Physio2U kinesiologist will collaborate with our physiotherapists to further enhance and support your rehabilitation and health goals.

03

Care Coordinators

To provide physiotherapy services that exceed expectations, the Physio2U therapists are supported by an operations team. This includes management and administrative staff with a variety of skill sets. The operations team acts with compassion, ensures provision of excellent customer service and creates a culture of continuous improvement so we can better help you.
